
Time units are used to represent the duration of time. The smallest unit of time is the second. Other time units are named after common units of time:

Minute: 60 seconds
Hour: 60 minutes
Day: 24 hours
Week: 7 days
Year: 365 days

You can use ether units and time units in your Solidity code to represent the value of an amount of ether or the duration of a period of time.
