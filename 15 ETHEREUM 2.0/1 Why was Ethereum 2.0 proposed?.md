Here are some of the specific reasons why Ethereum 2.0 was proposed:

Security: Ethereum 1.0 is vulnerable to attacks, such as the DAO attack in 2016. Ethereum 2.0 is designed to be more secure, with a number of new security features, such as sharding and Proof-of-Stake.
Energy efficiency: Ethereum 1.0 is a Proof-of-Work blockchain, which is energy-intensive. Ethereum 2.0 is designed to be more energy-efficient, with a Proof-of-Stake consensus mechanism.
